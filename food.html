Hungry?

Aventura

Aventura is an authentic Spanish restaurant serving Tapas, paella, wine & cocktails offered in a stylish, contemporary space with rustic touches.

Sava's

Sava's is a contemporary bi-level eatery that serves eclectic, locally sourced fare & a large Sunday brunch buffet.

Tomukun Korean BBQ

Tomukun Korean BBQ is an open dining space featuring Korean BBQ & a ramen noodle menu, plus Asian draft beers & spirits

Zingerman's Delicatessen

Zingerman's Delicatessen serves generous deli sandwiches at their funky, longtime market with specialty groceries.

Frita Batidos

Frita Batidos is a stark white space with picnic table seating serves colorful cuban street food & tropical cocktails.

Of Rice and Men

Of Rice and Men is an Asian fusion restaurant that takes on dim sum, sushi & other Asian classics featured in an upbeat, trendy eatery.

Mani Osteria

Mani Osteria serves wood-oven pizza & Italian small plates plus bar with old-world wines & local beer in an open setup.

Slurping Turtle

Slurping Turtle is an industrial-chic Japanese restaurant with long communal tables for tapas, noodles & sushi, plus sake.

Tios

Tios is a Mexican joint which offers late-night eats, a massive hot sauce collection & homemade salsa.
